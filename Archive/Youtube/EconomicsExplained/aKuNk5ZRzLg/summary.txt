- Singapore was recently ranked as the easiest country in the world to conduct business. It has made itself a central home of finance, business and trade for all of Southeast Asia. However, it has recently been usurped by New Zealand.
- Singapore's success is really a story of foreign investment. Over 70% of the country's total output is made up from foreign companies trading within the country. Singapore is a convenient location right at the centre of major shipping routes. These factors all combined to attract massive foreign investments.
